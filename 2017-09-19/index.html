Get a letter from someone who’s been in debt

You're not alone - although it can feel like that

Lots of people get into trouble with money. However, if you're in that situation, it can feel as though you’re the only one who has ever had a problem with debt.

Hear from people who have got themselves out of debt

There's a group of people who have been in debt and got out of it who want to help others in debt right now by writing letters to them.

Would you like to get a letter from one of these people?

They won’t be able to give you financial advice or recommend any particular course of action. What they will be able to do is show you that lots of people get into difficulties with debt, and lots of them end up sorting themselves out. They’ll be able to tell you how getting into debt felt - and how it felt to get out of it too.

A letter from someone who’s been there might be the inspiration you need to get yourself back on track.

I know how desperately isolating and terrifying it is to not have any money at all and to feel so desperate.

I got to some very dark places and I was so embarrassed.

I would love to explain what I went through and how much better I felt when I tackled it and faced my fears.

Helping people help each other

At Co-op we’re all about helping people help each other. We know that debt can make you feel isolated, and so we’re trying out letter writing as a way to someone reach out to a stranger in trouble who might not have anyone to talk to.

How it works

1. Get started

Let us know if you’d like to receive a letter from someone who’s been in debt before.

2. Get matched

We’ll match you to someone who’s been in a similar situation to the one you’re in now.

3. Get a letter

You don’t need to share your address with your letter writer, or even your second name, as the letter is sent to us and we’ll pass them on to you.

After you’ve received your letter, we’ll be in touch to ask you a few questions about how you found the experience.
